Why shorten a link?

Space-saving

Shortened links take up fewer characters, allowing you to make the most of limited space in text messages, tweets, social media posts, and other communication mediums.

Cleaner appearance

Long URLs can appear confusing and unattractive. By shortening links, you create a cleaner and more professional appearance, which can generate more user trust and engagement.

Easy sharing

Long links can be difficult to accurately copy and share. With shortened links, you facilitate sharing as they are shorter, easier to copy and paste, and less prone to errors.
